🌍✨ New Taipei ↔ Kaohsiung, Always on the Move!
I’m from Xinzhuang, New Taipei City, and spent my high school years at Xinzhuang High School 🎒. But for university, I moved south to study at National University of Kaohsiung 📚, officially starting my “dual-city life” between Taipei and Kaohsiung! 🚄💨 Every trip south feels like a mini adventure—having soy milk in Xinzhuang 🥤 in the morning and sipping papaya milk at Liuhe Night Market 🍹 by night.
